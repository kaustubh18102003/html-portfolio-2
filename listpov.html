BEST CINNAMON ROLLS BY KAUSTUBH

INGREDIANTS

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup of warm milk
  • 2/4 teaspoos yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups bread flour

for the filling

  • 2/3 cups of brown sugar
  • 1and1/2 table spoon ground cinamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix milk eith the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined with the dough.
  4. konced the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise it for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size,roll it out with a large rectangle,
  7. melt the butter for filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
